# 🛂 F1visa.Net ♾️

**Your F1 Journey, Together.**

F1visa.Net is the community-powered cooperative that helps international students navigate the US F1 visa process — transparently, affordably, together.

---

## 🌍 Who We Are

We're a member-owned cooperative built by and for international students. The F1 visa process is confusing, expensive, and isolating — we believe the collective wisdom of students who've been through it is more valuable than any paid consultancy.

| Value | Meaning |
|:------|:--------|
| 🌍 **Global Community** | 100+ countries, one cooperative |
| 🔒 **Trust & Accuracy** | Every piece of information is verified, sourced, and current |
| 💪 **Empowerment** | We equip students to take confident action |
| 🫶 **Mutual Support** | Every approved visa becomes knowledge shared back |
| ♾️ **Cooperative Ownership** | Members own the platform |
